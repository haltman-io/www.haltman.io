> ABOUT US

Haltman is a group of Brazilian hackers, but it wasn't initially created as "a group".

I bought the domain haltman.io and some time later I started using it as a pseudonym to separate my personal life, my professional life, and my parallel/public life.

I was fortunate to meet good and intelligent people at recent security conferences in Brazil.

The idea arose to create a group so we could help each other when needed, voluntary provisioning of services, guidance and infrastructure for members.

> DISCLAIMER

We are not affiliated with any other "hacker" group.
We're just a closed group of friends who got together here.
